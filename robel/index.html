Bonsai

A bonsai plant is a miniature tree shaped and pruned to stay small and artistically pleasing. It's a unique and carefully cultivated form of horticultural art, originating in China and Japan, known for its beauty and symbolism.

Orchids

Orchids are flowering plants with exotic, highly varied blooms. They come in a wide range of colors, shapes, and sizes, and can be found in tropical and subtropical regions all over the world.

cacuts

Cactus is succulent plants, meaning they have thick fleshy stems that store water. They are native to the Americas, with the greatest diversity in Mexico.

Snake plant

Snake plants are succulent houseplants with long, sword-shaped leaves. They are easy to care for and tolerant of low light and infrequent watering.

Peace lily

Peace lilies are tropical flowering plants with glossy, dark green leaves and white spathe flowers. They are known for their ability to thrive in low light conditions.
